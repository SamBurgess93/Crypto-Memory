How to play!

- The aim of the game is match all the 6 pairs. As the player you choose to flip over two cards at a time.

- If the two cards are not the same the cards flip back over. However remember their positions!!!

- If the two cards are the same, congratulations you found a match! Find all the pairs to complete the game

- An incorrect match is -20 points and a correct match is +50 points

- Good luck!
